Mischerade - Clothing & Design

As I delved deeper into the design process, I became more and more fascinated by the rich history and symbolism that lay at the heart of mythology. Every design element, from the font to the color scheme, was chosen to reflect the power and mystery of the ancient tales that still captivate the human imagination to this day.

Through the use of intricate patterns, bold graphics, and intricate details, I aimed to weave a tapestry of mythology that would take visitors on a journey through the ages. From the blazing suns of ancient Egypt to the mighty gods of Greece, every garment told a story and revealed secrets that had been passed down through the ages.

In essence, this project was not just about designing a shop, but about creating a space that celebrated the power of human imagination and the enduring legacy of mythology. It was a tribute to the stories and legends that have fascinated humanity for centuries, and a reminder of the magic and wonder that still exists in our world today.
