Ruang Ngopi - Social Media

As I immersed myself in the project, I couldn't help but be inspired by the vibrant energy of my client's coffee shop and its lush surroundings. The essence of the cafe's ethos was to offer a space for patrons to indulge in organic and wholesome treats while reveling in the tranquility of nature.

To capture the essence of the coffee shop's natural vibe, I turned to the color green - a symbol of growth, renewal, and vitality. Through the use of various shades of green in the design, I aimed to transport visitors to a world of natural beauty, where every sip of coffee and bite of pastry would be an ode to the earth.

Beyond the color scheme, I sought to create a design that was inclusive and inviting, drawing visitors of all ages and backgrounds to come together and share in the joys of life. From the font selection to the layout, every element of the design was crafted to foster a sense of community and connection among patrons.

In short, my design approach was a celebration of nature, community, and the simple pleasures of life - all woven together to create an unforgettable experience at the coffee shop.
