MyPertamina - UI/UX Study Case

The challenge of rebranding the UI & UX of the Pertamina app was like diving into the deep end of a vast ocean of digital design. The goal was to make the app not just visually appealing, but also more user-friendly and efficient in its functionality.

To achieve this, I meticulously reimagined every aspect of the app, from the font to the color scheme, with the end goal of making the functionality and visuals sharper and more intuitive for the user's eye.

With each design element, I aimed to strike the perfect balance between simplicity and sophistication, creating a sleek and modern aesthetic that would appeal to users of all ages and backgrounds. The result was a visually stunning app that not only looked great but was also easy to use and navigate.

From the smooth animations to the user-friendly layout, every detail was carefully considered to ensure that users would have a seamless experience when using the app. The end result was an app that not only met the client's goals but also exceeded the expectations of its users, making it a valuable tool for both consumers and the industry as a whole.

In conclusion, rebranding the UI & UX of the Pertamina app was a thrilling journey that required both creativity and technical skill. It was a project that challenged me to push the boundaries of design, all while keeping the needs and preferences of users at the forefront of my mind. The end result was an app that was not just beautiful to look at, but also a pleasure to use.
