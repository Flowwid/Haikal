Lokal Dimsum - Social Media

Embarking on the design journey for this Chinese cuisine project, I found myself captivated by the rich and vibrant culture that permeated every aspect of the cuisine. From the bold flavors to the intricate plating, every element was steeped in the traditions of China.

Drawing inspiration from the colorful 90s culture, I began crafting a design that would pay homage to the roots of Chinese cuisine while also adding a modern twist. The deep red background and bold fonts immediately drew the eye and evoked a sense of excitement and anticipation - much like the feeling one gets when anticipating a delicious meal.

However, as I delved deeper into the project, I realized that the owner of the restaurant had a desire to project an air of professionalism and sophistication. So, I pivoted my design approach to create a sleek and minimalist design that still made a bold statement. I chose to focus on one standout element that would grab attention and create a memorable impression - a clever design trick that elevated the overall impact of the project.

To keep the design visually interesting and prevent it from looking monotonous due to the extensive use of red, I experimented with subtle color gradations and shadow play, adding depth and dimension to the design. Ultimately, the final result was a stunning 1x3 design that was a seamless blend of traditional and contemporary aesthetics, showcasing the delicious cuisine in all its glory.
