Cloud Edge - Landing Page

This project was assigned by a company that is focusing on eco-friendly technology. the main request for the concept was to be simplistic & striking in a certain spot while showing informative facts through visuals of illustration. These requests are hard to grasp at the begging for this is a new experience for me, there are many errors and revisions throughout the project due to my lack of experience at that time.

In the end, I picked a neutral color for the background to show more of the product. As you can see, I use colorful and more detailed shapes inside the product to tell how breathtaking the quality of the camera of product, is because it is one of the selling points of the product. Everything from the left flow of the cloud going towards the product, to the neutral color for the fonts, background, and icon is to show and focus viewers' eyes toward the product
